ApheLux boutique is a small fashion business that started in 2020. The boutique sells stylish and affordable clothing for young women. it began with a few special items that customers liked a lot. As more people shopped at the boutique it added more clothes and accessories. In 2022 ApheLux opened a small store in Walmer Park near the shopping center where customers could come and shop comfortably.

Mission statement

ApheLux boutique’s mission is to provide stylish and affordable clothing that helps young women feel confident and express their own style. The boutique is committed to friendly service and a good shopping experience.

Vission Statement

ApheLux boutique’s vision is to grow into a well known local fashion brand that continues to inspire confidence and make shopping easy and enjoyable for all customers.
